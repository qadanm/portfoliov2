Research into multi-agent workflow design: what context needs to be preserved at handoff points, how to structure inter-agent communication, and where human review adds the most value in a pipeline.

Current focus: the relationship between agent specialization and handoff overhead. Highly specialized agents produce better output but require more careful context passing. General agents are more forgiving but less precise.

**Status:** Ongoing research, applied to personal projects.
